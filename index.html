ABOUT OUR PLACEMENT & TRAINING - HEAL THE WORLD

The AAPL course is a multi-level recreational course in English Language training/soft-skills for the school and college students. The training covers all four language skills: orientation, filtering, personal interview and job offering, as well as improving pronunciation and building vocabulary as per the requirement of the company. Particular emphasis is placed on various concerns including Basic professionalism, Emotion regulation, Growth mindset, Self-confidence, Stress management, Resilience, Forgive and forgot, Persistence and perseverance, Patience, Perceptiveness, etc.

AAPL’s placements are interactive, recreational and 100% job assured concern. Where the students are motivated to interact with their counterpart, participate in many skills exhibiting activities, group discussions etc

Special emphasis on training session would be on simple conversation, situational language, vocabulary building, grammar, pronunciation, etiquette, manners and other aspects of communication which go a long way developing the overall personality of students. Based on the progress shown by the students, they would be trained in public speaking, body language, Presentation skills, personality development, Group discussions,personal interviews etc.
